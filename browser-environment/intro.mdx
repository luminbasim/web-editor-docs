Many workflows will involve browser navigation (e.g. opening a new tab, closing a tab, navigating to a url) or interacting with the DOM (e.g. getting the text of selected element(s) or clicking on selected element(s)).

When running a workflow, the engine keeps track of the history of the browser tabs a workflow has created in the `workflow’s browser environment`. In this section we will discuss the two most used/referenced tabs in a workflow’s browser environment.
